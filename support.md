## 2. Common Issues & Solutions
### Sign in with Apple / Google not working
- Ensure you have enabled Sign in with Apple in your device settings
- Check that your internet connection is stable
- Verify the privacy policy URL is correctly configured

### Alarm not ringing
- Make sure the app has permission to send notifications
- Check Do Not Disturb mode is not active
- Verify your device's alarm/notification settings
